:line

volume command :h3

[Syntax:]

volume V :pre

V = volume of simulation domain (liters) :ul

[Examples:]

volume 8.0e-15

[Description:]

Set the volume of the system for a Gillepsie (non-spatial) simulation.

[Restrictions:] none
